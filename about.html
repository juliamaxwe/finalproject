Homemade in Ohio

Jen Maxwell starting making cookies for friends and family in 2016. As word of her delicious cookies spread, demand increased. This gave birth to Big Sugar Sweets, Jen's home bakery in Mansfield, Ohio. Now, Big Sugar does a full range of designed, gourmet sugar cookies. Jen is always working to hone her skills and develop new artistic designs for any event.

Please contact us to recieve a quote, place an order, or ask a question!

Pricing

Cookies prices are based on the complexity of the design, the amount of colors, and the amount of cookie cutters/hand cutting required.

Basic Cookies

Basic cookies start at $36.00 per dozen. These are two colors, non-complex designs, and 2-3 shapes.

Premium Cookies

Premium cookies start at $48.00 per dozen. These are three or more colors, more than three shapes, some hand painting, stenciling, or airbrushing.

Deluxe Cookies

Deluxe cookies are $55.00+ per dozen. Deluxe cookies have multiple colors and shapes, hand cut designs, hand painting, metallics, stenciling, or airbrushing.

Other Pricing

Miniature cookies are $24.00+ per dozen. Any cookies with logos are $48.00+ per dozen.
