Frequently Asked Questions

Can you ship me my cookies?

We can't ship, unfortunately, because of Ohio's Cottage Laws regarding home bakeries.

What is the allergy information for the cookies?

Our cookies contain gluten, nut, egg, and dairy products. We can make gluten free cookies for a slightly higher price, but we are not a gluten free or nut free kitchen.

How do I get my cookies?

You can come pick them up, or we will deliver them if you live in the Mansfield area!
